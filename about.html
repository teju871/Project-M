Welcome to Merritt Innovative Solutions

Merritt Innovative Solutions India Pvt. Ltd. (Merritt), is an ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 27001 certified engineering services specialist company having deep down expertise in product development on a global delivery model. Team brings in decades of experience and knowledge in product design and development for verticals like Off-Highway, Transportation, e-Mobility, Industrial/IOT products, Consumer products, Aerospace and Defense. In last 11 years, team developed 130+ new products that involves design, proto build, test & prove out, manufacturing and supply.
