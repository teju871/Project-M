Material handling

Material handling acquired greater importance due to the growing need to reduce manufacturing and handling costs. We recognise that a good process layout with a material handling system will determine the success of lean material handling.

MERRITT as a leading engineering service provider of conveyors design, special Equipment's and customized material handling equipment to suit specific requirements. Systems designed by MERRITT are the centrepiece of several Indian industries as well as Industries abroad.

We have expertise in designing and developing conveyors, special-purpose machines (SPM), and customised material handling equipment to suit specific requirements in automotive, bulk, and warehouse operations.

Merritt has hands-on experience in the design and development of the following material-handling equipment:

1. Skillets

  • Rollers & Camrol
  • Skillet positioner
  • Perch details

2. Transfers

  • Belt shuttle
  • Cross transfer
  • Power roll bed

3. Lifts

  • Vertical drive lift
  • Auxiliary lift
  • Fork transfer
  • Scissor lift

4. Drives Section

  • Drive encoder
  • Counter pressure roller

5. Turntables

6. Electrical monorail
